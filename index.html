About me

I have over 25 years of professional experience in the Cybersecurity sector. Throughout my career, I have worked in international organizations, where I have participated in relevant technological transformation projects in various sectors such as banking, public administration, insurance, IBEX35 companies, among many others.

Throughout my career, I have sought to specialize in security management, both from a technological and strategic point of view. I consider myself a multidisciplinary profile, oriented to the client, together with an understanding of technologies at a very low level, this has allowed me to become an interface capable of understanding technology and business. Being very useful to always collaborate with senior management as a strategic advisor.

My professional career has led me to live in cities such as Valencia, Barcelona, Madrid and Rome, as well as to actively travel to countries such as Germany, Italy, Israel and the USA to learn about new security products and services from the most outstanding startups in this sector

In the companies where I have acted as a manager, I have tried to help in the analysis, management and mitigation of cybersecurity risks, always applying the best standards, best practices and controls to always provide adequate protection to the information, services and systems of the organization
